# Medical Info Setup

Walk the user through setting up medical information for the family. Create entries in the "Medical" category. Ask who to set up first, then repeat for each family member.

## Per Family Member
Create an entry titled "[Name] — Medical Info" for each person with:

### Primary Doctor
- Doctor name and practice
- Phone number
- Patient portal URL and login
- Address

### Specialists (if any)
- For each specialist: name, specialty, phone, address

### Medications
- Current medications: name, dosage, frequency, prescribing doctor
- Pharmacy name, phone, and address
- Pharmacy account/member number

### Allergies
- Drug allergies (and reactions)
- Food allergies
- Environmental allergies
- Allergy severity (mild, moderate, severe/anaphylaxis)

### Medical History
- Blood type
- Chronic conditions
- Past surgeries (date, procedure, hospital)
- Immunization status (up to date? any gaps?)

**Sensitive fields:** Patient portal login, pharmacy account number

## Health Insurance
Create an entry titled "Health Insurance" with:
- Insurance company and plan name
- Policy number and group number
- Primary policyholder
- Phone number for claims
- In-network vs out-of-network deductibles
- Copay amounts (PCP, specialist, ER, urgent care)
- Prescription coverage details

**Sensitive fields:** Policy number, group number

## Dental & Vision
Create an entry titled "Dental & Vision Insurance" with:
- Dental insurance: company, policy number, dentist name/phone
- Vision insurance: company, policy number, optometrist name/phone
- Coverage details (cleanings, exams, glasses/contacts)

## Emergency Info
Create an entry titled "Emergency Medical Info" with:
- Nearest hospital name, address, ER phone
- Nearest urgent care name, address, phone
- Poison control: 1-800-222-1222
- Family member emergency contacts
- Any advance directives or medical power of attorney (note where originals are stored)
