# Emergency Contacts Setup

Walk the user through creating a comprehensive emergency contact list. Create entries in the "Personal" category.

## Emergency Contacts
Create an entry titled "Emergency Contacts" with:

### ICE (In Case of Emergency)
- ICE Contact 1: name, relationship, phone (cell), phone (work)
- ICE Contact 2: name, relationship, phone (cell), phone (work)
- ICE Contact 3: name, relationship, phone (cell)

### Nearby Help
- Nearest trusted neighbor: name, address, phone
- Backup neighbor: name, address, phone
- Local family member (if nearby): name, phone

### Emergency Services
- Local police non-emergency: phone
- Fire department non-emergency: phone
- Poison Control: 1-800-222-1222
- Nearest hospital ER: name, address, phone
- Nearest urgent care: name, address, phone, hours
- Family doctor after-hours line: phone

### Utilities Emergency
- Electric company emergency line
- Gas company emergency/leak line
- Water company emergency line
- Plumber (emergency/24hr): name, phone
- Electrician (emergency): name, phone

### Kids-Specific
- Pediatrician after-hours: phone
- Preferred babysitter: name, phone
- Backup babysitter: name, phone
- School emergency contacts on file (confirm they match this list)

### Pets (if applicable)
- Veterinarian: name, phone, address
- Emergency vet (24hr): name, phone, address
- Pet sitter/boarding: name, phone

## Important Numbers Card
Create an entry titled "Wallet Emergency Card" with a summary of the most critical numbers:
- Suggest the user print this as a small card for their wallet
- ICE 1 phone, ICE 2 phone, doctor, hospital, home address, blood types, allergies
